Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Baby Signal app collects the following types of information:

  • Identifiers: Device ID, advertising ID
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, analysis history
  • Diagnostics: App performance and crash reports
  • User Content: Audio recordings (processed only on device)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Third-Party Advertising: To display Google AdMob advertisements
  • Analytics: To measure app performance and improve user experience
  • App Functionality: For baby cry analysis and subscription management

3. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising display and targeting
  • Apple: For subscription management and payment processing

4. Data Security

Your audio recordings are processed only on your device and are never sent to our servers. Other data is encrypted and stored securely.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to data processing

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents can control their children's data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated. Significant changes will be notified within the app.
